Abstract

This longitudinal study examines "micromance" – defined as brief, frequent digital interactions (≤5 minutes) that cumulatively build emotional intimacy in dating app contexts. Through mixed-methods analysis of 2,150 active dating app users over 12 months, we investigate how micro-interactions challenge traditional courtship timelines.

Research Questions

RQ1

Do brief digital interactions (≤5 minutes) produce emotional intimacy comparable to traditional extended interactions?

RQ2

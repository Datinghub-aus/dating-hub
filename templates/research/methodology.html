Methodological Philosophy

Our research follows a 4-phase framework designed to ensure scientific rigor, ethical compliance, and practical relevance in dating and relationship studies. We combine quantitative and qualitative methods with transparent reporting standards.

Transparency

All methodologies are fully documented and available for peer review

Reproducibility

Studies can be independently verified using our published protocols

Ethical Integrity

All research complies with international ethical standards

Practical Relevance

Findings are grounded in real-world dating experiences

4-Phase Research Framework

Systematic approach from hypothesis formulation to publication and replication

1

Design & Protocol Development

Formulating research questions, designing study protocols, and obtaining ethical approval.

2

Data Collection & Validation

Implementing rigorous data collection methods with continuous quality validation.

3

Analysis & Interpretation

Applying statistical and qualitative analysis with multiple validation checks.

4

Publication & Replication

Transparent reporting and supporting independent replication studies.

Research Methods

Quantitative Methods

  • Large-scale surveys (1,000–5,000 participants)
  • Statistical analysis of behavioral data
  • Longitudinal tracking studies
  • Regression and correlation analysis

Qualitative Methods

  • In-depth interviews (45–90 minutes)
  • Focus group discussions
  • Content analysis of user communications
  • Thematic and narrative analysis

Ethical Standards

Participant Protection

All research undergoes ethics review. Participants provide informed consent, data is anonymized, and sensitive information is protected with encryption.

Data Security

All personal data is encrypted, stored securely, and only accessible to authorized research team members.

Transparency & Accountability

Full methodology disclosure, conflict of interest management, and independent review board oversight.

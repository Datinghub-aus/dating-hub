1. Introduction & Overview

Our approach is grounded in pragmatic mixed-methods philosophy, prioritizing methodological rigor, transparency, and real-world applicability.

Precision

Statistical rigor and validity.

Depth

Contextual understanding.

Integration

Multi-angle analysis.

Transparency

Full disclosure.

2. Quantitative Research Standards

2.1 Survey Design

  • Pilot Testing: All instruments tested with 50-100 participants.
  • Validation: Psychometric reliability testing (α > 0.70).

2.2 Sampling

  • Power Analysis: Minimum standards (1-β ≥ 0.80, α = 0.05).
  • Balancing: Quota sampling used for demographics.

3. Qualitative Research Standards

In-depth Interviews

Semi-structured interviews with verbatim transcription.

Duration: 45-90 minutes

Sample: 15-30 per study

Focus Groups

Trained facilitators using assistant moderators.

Size: 6-8 participants

Groups: 4-6 per study

Ethnography

Naturalistic observation in digital dating environments.

Type: Passive and Participant

Analysis: Thick description

5. Quality Assurance Protocols

Data Validation

10% random sample re-analysis.

Peer Review

Internal board and external experts.

Consistency

Automated outlier examination.

Transparency

Code sharing and data availability.

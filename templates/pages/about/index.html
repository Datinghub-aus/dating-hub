About Dating Hub

Data-driven insights for modern relationships across Australia.

Our Mission

To transform how Australians understand and navigate modern relationships through rigorous, evidence-based research that bridges academic insight with real-world application.

Our Vision

A future where relationship decisions are informed by data, where patterns are understood through research, and where Australians have access to transparent insights about modern dating.

What We Do

Original Research

Conducting large-scale studies on dating behaviors, platform usage, and relationship trends across Australia.

Data Tools

Developing practical tools like our AI Matchmaker to help individuals make informed relationship decisions.

Trend Analysis

Tracking and analyzing emerging patterns in digital dating, communication styles, and relationship dynamics.

Our Approach

Ethical Rigor

All research undergoes independent ethics review. We prioritize participant privacy, data security, and transparent methodology.

Methodological Excellence

Combining quantitative precision with qualitative depth using validated instruments and peer-reviewed protocols.

Real-World Relevance

Research designed to address practical questions faced by Australians navigating modern dating landscapes.

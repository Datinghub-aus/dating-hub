Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect research data and website visitor information.

1. Scope & Application

This Privacy Policy governs data handling practices for Dating Hub Research ("we," "us," or "our") across all research activities, website interactions, and data processing operations. It applies to:

  • Research participants in our studies
  • Website visitors and users
  • Academic collaborators and data requesters
  • Newsletter subscribers and research updates recipients

2. Research Data Collection & Processing

Our primary data collection occurs through voluntary research participation. We adhere to strict ethical guidelines:

2.1 Participant Data Collection

  • Voluntary Participation: All research participation requires informed consent
  • Anonymization Protocol: Personal identifiers are removed during data processing
  • Data Categories: We collect demographic, behavioral, and attitudinal data relevant to dating and relationship research
  • Storage Duration: Research data is retained for 7 years as per academic standards

2.2 Data Protection Measures

Protection Measure Description Implementation
Encryption All sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit AES-256 encryption standard
Access Controls Role-based access to research data Minimum necessary principle
Data Minimization Collect only essential data for research objectives Study-specific data collection protocols
Regular Audits Quarterly security and privacy assessments Internal and external audits

3. Website Analytics & Tracking

We use limited analytics to understand website usage while respecting visitor privacy:

Note: We do not use cross-site tracking, behavioral advertising, or third-party cookies.

3.1 Cookies & Local Storage

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality (session management)
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage statistics (IP anonymized)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember user settings (language, theme)
  • Opt-Out: Cookie preferences can be managed in browser settings

3.2 Third-Party Services

We use limited third-party services with strong privacy commitments:

  • Analytics: Self-hosted Plausible Analytics (GDPR compliant)
  • Hosting: Australian-based servers with local data storage
  • Email: Encrypted email services for research communications

4. Data Sharing & Publication

Research data is shared responsibly following academic standards:

4.1 Academic Sharing

  • Anonymized datasets available for academic research
  • Data sharing agreements required for sensitive datasets
  • Attribution required: "Dating Hub Research [Year]. [Dataset Name]"
  • Commercial use requires separate licensing agreements

4.2 Publication Standards

All published research undergoes privacy review to ensure:

  • No personally identifiable information in publications
  • Statistical disclosure control for small sample sizes
  • Aggregate reporting to prevent re-identification
  • Ethical review board approval for all studies

5. Your Rights & Controls

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data where applicable
  • Restriction: Request limitations on data processing
  • Portability: Request data in structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
Research Participants: Please contact us using your study participant ID for data-related requests.

6. International Data Transfers

As an Australian-based research organization with international collaborators:

  • Primary data storage in Australian data centers
  • International transfers only with adequate safeguards
  • Standard Contractual Clauses for EU/UK data transfers
  • Cross-border research collaborations with data protection agreements

7. Children's Privacy

Our research focuses on adult populations (18+ years):

  • We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18
  • Age verification in research participant screening
  • Website content intended for adult audiences
  • Parental consent required for any exceptional youth-focused research

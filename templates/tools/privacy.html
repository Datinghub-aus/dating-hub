Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect research data and website visitor information.

1. Scope & Application

This Privacy Policy governs data handling practices for All Aces Australia operating Dating Hub Research ("we," "us," or "our") across all research activities, website interactions, and data processing operations. It applies to research participants, website visitors, academic collaborators, and newsletter subscribers.

2. Data Collection

We collect information necessary for academic-standard research and service improvement:

  • Research Data: Survey responses, behavioral data, and demographic information.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and usage patterns (see our Terms for cookie details).
  • Contact Data: Email addresses for newsletters or research participation.

3. Use of Information

We use collected information for specific, lawful purposes:

  • To conduct academic-style research on dating behaviors and preferences
  • To personalise and improve your experience with our AI-powered tools and surveys
  • To communicate with you about research findings, updates, and newsletters (with consent)
  • To ensure the security and proper functioning of our website

4. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose personal information to:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., email delivery, hosting, analytics) who assist our operations
  • Academic or research partners in anonymised, aggregated form only
  • Government authorities if required by Australian law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Your Rights & Choices

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or out-of-date information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time
  • Make a complaint about our handling of your personal information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@dating-hub.com.au.

6. Security & Data Retention

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your information. We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by Australian law.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For detailed information, please refer to the Cookies section of our Terms of Use.
